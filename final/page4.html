The Jump

You attempt to jump over the hole. You take a running start and muster all your strength, eyes fixed on the ledge across the gap. Time seems to slow down as your feet leave the ground.

For a moment, it feels like you might make it. But then, your fingers graze the edge, just short. As you fall you think, "if only I took the other path".

Suddenly, you're engulfed in heat and you descend into a pool of molten lava.

There is no escape this time.
